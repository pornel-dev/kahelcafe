Our Story

About Kahel

Born from Sunshine

A Café Rooted in
Warmth & Community

Kahel — the Filipino word for orange — was born in 2018 from a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee can turn an ordinary moment into something extraordinary.

Founded by two friends who spent years exploring coffee culture across Southeast Asia, Kahel is our love letter to community, craft, and the warm Filipino spirit.

Every detail — from our citrus-forward blends to our sun-drenched interiors — is designed to make you feel at home.

Sustainably Sourced
Direct trade relationships with farmers across Benguet, Sagada, and Mt. Apo.
House Roasted
We roast in small batches weekly to ensure peak freshness in every cup.
Community First
10% of profits go to local farmer cooperatives and youth programs.
Locally Inspired
Interiors, menu, and events shaped by Filipino culture and creativity.

The Method

From Bean to Cup —
Our 5-Step Craft

01
Source

Direct trade from Benguet, Sagada & Mt. Apo farmers. We visit every harvest season.

02
Roast

Small-batch weekly roasting in-house. We target light-to-medium profiles for citrus clarity.

03
Extract

Every shot pulled at 9 bars, 93°C, 25-30 seconds. Dialed in fresh each morning.

04
Steam

Micro-foam textured at 60-65°C. We use local oat milk as the default for creaminess.

05
Serve

Every cup leaves with intention — a latte art rosette or simple elegance, always with care.
